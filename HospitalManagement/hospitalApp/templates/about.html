About Us

Our disease prediction system is a web-based platform that allows users to predict their chances of having breast cancer, diabetes, and heart disease based on their symptoms, medical history, and other relevant information.

Our system is designed to provide users with an accurate and reliable diagnosis, and to help them make informed decisions about their health. We use advanced algorithms and machine learning techniques to analyze large amounts of data and provide personalized predictions for each user.

Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the breast tissue. It is the most common type of cancer among women, but it can also occur in men. Our system uses a variety of factors to predict a user's risk of breast cancer, including age, family history, and lifestyle factors such as diet and exercise.

Diabetes

Diabetes is a condition in which the body is unable to regulate blood sugar levels. There are two main types of diabetes: type 1 diabetes and type 2 diabetes. Our system takes into account a user's age, weight, family history, and other factors to predict their risk of developing diabetes.

Heart Disease

Heart disease refers to a range of conditions that affect the heart, including coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmias. Our system uses a variety of factors to predict a user's risk of heart disease, including age, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and lifestyle factors such as smoking and exercise.
